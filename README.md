Graduation Clearance System
A web-based Graduation Clearance System developed to automate and streamline the student graduation clearance process. The system enables students to complete departmental clearance online while allowing administrators and department heads to efficiently manage approvals, reports, and graduation readiness.

Overview
The Graduation Clearance System replaces the traditional paper-based clearance process with a secure, centralized, and workflow-driven platform. Students complete clearance by following a predefined sequence of departments, while department heads review, approve, or reject applications electronically.

Clearance Workflow
Students complete clearance in the following order:
1.	Computer Laboratory
2.	Student Guild Council
3.	Hostel Management
4.	Dean of Students Office
5.	University Library
6.	Facilities Management
7.	Finance Department
8.	Directorate of Quality Assurance
9.	Academic Department / Head of Department
10.	Academic Registry
Departments are unlocked one at a time. A student cannot proceed to the next department until the current department has approved their clearance.
